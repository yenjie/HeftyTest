1. **T-matrix analysis of static Wilson line correlators from lattice QCD at finite temperature**
   Authors: **Zhanduo Tang, Swagato Mukherjee, Peter Petreczky, Ralf Rapp**.
   First posted on **October 29, 2023**, later published in **EPJ A 60, 92 (2024)**. This is the key paper where the thermodynamic T-matrix framework is constrained using **2+1-flavor lattice-QCD Wilson line correlators**. ([arXiv][3])

2. **Bottomonium Properties in QGP from a Lattice-QCD Informed T-Matrix Approach**
   Authors: **Zhanduo Tang, Swagato Mukherjee, Peter Petreczky, Ralf Rapp**.
   Posted on **November 14, 2024**. This paper adds **bottomonium extended-operator correlators** from lattice QCD and refines the in-medium potential and interference effects. I did not find a confirmed journal publication page for it, so I would currently treat it as a **preprint**. ([arXiv][4])

3. **Quarkonium Spectroscopy in the Quark-Gluon Plasma**
   Authors: **Zhanduo Tang, Biaogang Wu, Andrew Hanlon, Swagato Mukherjee, Peter Petreczky, Ralf Rapp**.
   Posted on **February 13, 2025**. This extends the program by locating **complex-energy T-matrix poles** and defining melting through the disappearance of those poles. ([arXiv][2])

4. **Non-perturbative quarkonium dissociation rates in strongly coupled quark-gluon plasma**
   Authors: **Biaogang Wu, Zhanduo Tang, Ralf Rapp**.
   Posted on **March 13, 2025** and published in **JHEP 07 (2025) 162**. This paper uses the same **lattice-QCD-constrained kernel** to compute quarkonium dissociation rates. ([arXiv][5])

5. **Bottomonium transport in a strongly coupled quark-gluon plasma**
   Authors: **Biaogang Wu, Ralf Rapp**.
   Posted on **August 28, 2025** and published in **Physics Letters B 873 (2026) 140223**. This is the latest transport application built on the lattice-constrained T-matrix reaction rates. ([arXiv][1])
